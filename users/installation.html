Installation¶

Prerequisites¶

Phynx requires several packages in order to run:

  • Python (version 2.5 or 2.6)
  • h5py (1.2.1 or later)
  • Phynx

Phynx developers should also install:

  • distribute (version 0.6.3 or later)
  • sphinx (version 0.6.3 or later)
  • nose (version 0.11.1 or later)

Development Branch¶

You can follow and contribute to Phynx’s development by obtaining a bzr version control branch. Just install bzr and type:

bzr branch lp:phynx

and then periodically bring your branch up to date:

bzr pull

Bugs, feature requests, and questions can be directed to the launchpad website.
